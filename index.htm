Welcome to FLAMES Calculator! A trip back to your childhood!!!!
FLAMES stands for :
F - Friend
L - Love
A - Affection
M - Marriage
E - Enemies
S - Siblings

Flames is a funny game which determines relationship between two people based on the number of common letters of their name. The relationship is obviously HYPOTHETICAL.

This Website is only for fun. This FLAMES calculator does NOT determine the actual relationship.
